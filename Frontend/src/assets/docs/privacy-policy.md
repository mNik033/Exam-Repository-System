# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 15 July 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the Exam Repository System ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our platform for uploading, sharing, discovering university exam papers, and generating AI-assisted answers (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or is reasonably capable of being associated with you ("Personal Information") in the following ways:

### A. Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information:** When you register for an account, we collect your full name, institutional email address (verified via a One-Time Password), and a password. Your password is encrypted and stored as a bcrypt hash; we never store or log plaintext passwords. You may also provide a referral code during signup.
- **Academic Preferences & Browsing History:** During registration or via your profile settings, we collect a list of courses you are enrolled in. We also store a history of your recently browsed courses to provide personalized recommendations.
- **Uploaded Content:** When you upload exam papers, we collect the files (which may be in image or PDF format) and the content contained within them. If you upload one or more images, our upload tool automatically converts and compiles them into a single PDF document client-side within your browser; the separate raw images are not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
- **Payment Information:** If you choose to purchase additional platform credits, we collect information related to your purchase. **Note:** We do not collect or store raw payment instruments, credit card numbers, or bank details. All payment processing is handled securely by our third-party payment processor, Razorpay. We only store transaction-related metadata (e.g., Razorpay order ID, payment ID, signature, purchase amount, and credit quantity) to verify your transaction and update your account balance.

### B. Information Generated by Your Use of the Platform
- **System-Generated Account Data:** We automatically generate and store certain data to manage your account, including a unique user identifier, an auto-generated alphanumeric referral code, your current credit balance, a history of answers you have unlocked, and a log of in-app notifications (including message content, type, read status, associated paper ID, and timestamps).
- **File Hashes:** We generate and store a BLAKE3 cryptographic hash of the content of any file you upload. This hash is used exclusively for detecting duplicate uploads and preventing redundant processing. It is stored temporarily in our upload registry along with upload metadata (timestamp, uploader ID, processing status, and subscriber list).
- **AI-Generated Content:** As part of our Service, uploaded exam papers are processed by artificial intelligence (specifically, the Google Gemini API). This processing extracts structured data including question text, answer text, topic tags, course IDs, sessions, session years, exam types, and paper titles. We also generate vector embeddings of the extracted questions to deduplicate similar or identical questions via MongoDB's Exact Nearest Neighbor (ENN) capabilities during the upload process. This processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, as detailed in Section 4.
- **Aggregated System Metrics:** We collect and compile aggregated, anonymous usage and system performance metrics (such as counts of answers unlocked, total uploads processed, and validation failure reasons) for infrastructure tracking, capacity planning, database optimization, and future enhancements. These metrics do not contain personal identifiers.
- **Real-Time Connection Metadata:** When you open a live connection to our real-time notification service, we transiently process connection metadata (such as your IP address, connection duration, and user ID) to route alerts via Redis. This data is handled ephemerally.
- **Security Logs and IP Address Processing:** To protect the Service from abuse, scraping, and cyber threats, our security middleware monitors incoming traffic. We collect and evaluate IP addresses for global rate limiting and automated banning (which blocks access for 24 hours after multiple security violations). Suspected malicious activities and violating IP addresses are persisted in a security log file on our server.

### C. Verification Data
- **One-Time Passwords (OTPs):** When you sign up or verify your email, we temporarily store your email address and a 6-digit OTP code in our database. This data is strictly used for verification purposes and is automatically permanently deleted upon expiry.

### D. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Currently, the Exam Repository System does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking pixels to monitor your behavior across the web. However, we do utilize your browser's HTML5 Local Storage strictly to maintain active, secure sessions (e.g., storing your authentication tokens) and to remember your user interface preferences (e.g., dark mode). These mechanisms are strictly necessary for the platform to function and are not used for cross-site tracking.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for various business and operational purposes, including to:
- **Provide and Maintain the Service:** To authenticate your identity, manage your account, authorize access to features, and process credit transactions.
- **Process Uploads:** To analyze uploaded files via AI, extract structured exam content, and organize the repository.
- **Ensure Platform Integrity:** To compare file hashes against our database to detect and reject duplicate file uploads, ensuring the repository remains clean and awarding credits accurately.
- **Ensure Content Uniqueness:** To utilize vector embeddings for deduplicating identical or semantically similar questions using MongoDB's Exact Nearest Neighbor (ENN) indexing, keeping the repository clean during paper processing.
- **Personalize Your Experience:** To use your enrolled and recently browsed courses to tailor the dashboard and recommend relevant exam papers.
- **Communicate with You:** To send you in-app notifications regarding the status of your uploads (e.g., approved, rejected, duplicate) and deliver essential account-related emails (e.g., OTPs).
- **Manage the Credit Economy:** To track your credit balance, award credits for valid uploads, and deduct credits when you unlock AI-generated answers.

## 4. How We Share and Disclose Your Information
We do not sell your Personal Information. We share your information only with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements. 

Our current third-party integrations include:
- **Google Gemini API:** We transmit the content of the files you upload (images or PDFs) to Google Gemini to extract questions, generate answers, and create vector embeddings. During analysis, these files are temporarily hosted on Google's API infrastructure and are deleted automatically and immediately from Google's environment once the extraction task completes. This data processing is subject to [Google's API Terms of Service](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms) and the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google Workspace / Gmail:** We use Google's SMTP infrastructure to send transactional emails, such as One-Time Passwords (OTPs) for account verification. Your email address is processed by Google to deliver these messages.
- **Razorpay:** When you initiate a credit purchase, we share your email address and the order amount with Razorpay to facilitate the transaction. Payment processing is subject to the [Razorpay Terms of Use](https://razorpay.com/terms/) and the [Razorpay Privacy Policy](https://razorpay.com/privacy/).
- **Cloudflare R2:** Uploaded exam paper files are securely stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage. This storage is subject to the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
- **MongoDB Atlas:** Our primary database is hosted on MongoDB Atlas, which stores account data, metadata, and extracted text. This hosting is subject to the [MongoDB Privacy Policy](https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Vercel (Frontend Hosting):** Our web application frontend is hosted on Vercel. When you access the website, Vercel may collect standard connection data (such as IP addresses) necessary to serve the web application. This is subject to the [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Content Delivery Networks (CDNs):** Our frontend utilizes third-party CDNs (such as Google Fonts) to serve user interface assets dynamically. When your browser requests these assets, your IP address and connection metadata are necessarily exposed to those providers to download the files, which is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Data Storage, Security, and Infrastructure
We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.
- **Infrastructure:** Our web application frontend is hosted on Vercel. Our backend services run on a combination of Oracle Cloud Virtual Machines, Termux instances, and potentially other hosting platforms (e.g., Render) as we scale horizontally. Internal networking and scaling may utilize VPN services such as Tailscale. Our caching and rate-limiting infrastructure utilizes Redis. We reserve the right to migrate our hosting, database, or network infrastructure to different vendors at any time without prior notice as our operational needs evolve.
- **Security Protections:** The Service implements automated firewalls that block incoming traffic originating from known cloud hosting provider IP ranges (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure) to prevent unauthorized bot activity, automated scrapers, and denial-of-service attempts.
- **Encryption:** All user passwords are cryptographically hashed using the bcrypt algorithm before storage. We use stateless JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) for session management, ensuring no active session data is stored server-side.
- **Security Limitation:** While we strive to protect your Personal Information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
- **Data Breach Notification:** In the event that any Personal Information under our control is compromised as a result of a breach of security, we will take reasonable steps to investigate the situation and, where appropriate, notify those individuals whose information may have been compromised in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## 6. Data Retention
We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:
- **Account Data:** Retained indefinitely while your account remains active. Upon requesting account deletion, your data may be retained for a grace period of up to 7 days before being permanently deleted.
- **Uploaded Content & Extracted Data:** Exam papers, extracted questions, and AI-generated content are retained indefinitely as part of the shared academic repository, even if the original uploader deletes their account.
- **Verification Data (OTPs):** Database records of OTPs are stored temporarily and automatically deleted upon expiry using MongoDB Time-To-Live (TTL) indexes. However, please note that the generated OTPs are transmitted via email and will naturally remain in our sent email logs.
- **Upload Registry Data:** File hashes used to track active upload processing are deleted shortly after processing completes or fails. However, the subscriber lists associated with those uploads may be retained.
- **Cache Data:** Redis cache entries (such as rate limit counters) are ephemeral and expire automatically based on predefined TTL settings.
- **Aggregated Metrics:** Fully anonymized system performance metrics are retained indefinitely for analytical and optimization purposes.
- **Real-Time Connection Data:** Socket-level routing data and stream metadata are held ephemerally and cleared immediately upon disconnection.
- **Security Logs:** Entries in our security log file, including logged IP addresses and associated security events, are retained for audit and defense purposes and are periodically purged.
